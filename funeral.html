Funeral — 2025

Documentation photographs of a collective performance at the church of Billingsfors, Sweden. The metal log used in the performance is a part of the work Log Swing.

A sunday afternoon, it's quiet, the chimneys of the pulp factory are running at full capacity, they never rest. A log carried through the cemetery, giving the village life, giving the village death.
